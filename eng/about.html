About

low-price,
high-quality

Fair Sun Industrial Co. Ltd. was established in 1980 specialized in Automotive Switches, Door Lock Actuators, Solenoids, Relays, Clock Springs, Sensors, and other electrical auto parts to cover many different car brands. For over 30 years, Fair Sun has been focused on applying the best production technologies to maintain our customers satisfaction with the highest quality standards.

With over 30 years manufacturing experience and IAFT16949 certification, Fair Sun has became Tier 1 supplier to OE/OES customers in North America and Europe from 2004.


During 30 years we have been always improving our technical procedures from our R&D department and also the skills to obtain the best from the most advanced equipments acquired by Fair Sun. From the initial study of the products drawing to the final production and assembly we go through all the process improvement such us Computer Assisted Design improvement, mold finishing, product durability testing, etc… Different lab testing procedures take place continuously in the different product stages creating the teamwork essential for the success of each final products. Also the direct cooperation between Taiwan Fair Sun factory and Shanghai Fairsun factory allow us to produce the same high quality standards in China as the ones we have been providing to our customers for the last 30 years but with even more competitive prices.

Shanghai Fairsun Electronic Co. Ltd., was established in 1999 as the Fairsun Industrial Taiwan Co. Ltd. subsidiary in China specialized in the auto parts production for worldwide export.

3-LEVEL QUALITY POLICY

Our goal is to supply the highest quality products, not only emphasizing and improving production processes but also with the best techniques to use the most updated QC systems. All our products are TQC (Total Quality Control). We are not satisfied with the actual business situation and steady growth for the last 30 years, we are already developing new products and continuously improving our processes to continue winning market share and satisfying customers all over the world.

Our main products are Automotive Switches, Door Lock Actuators, Solenoids, Relays, Clock Springs, Sensors, etc… Main markets are North America, Europe and Middle East other markets we are currently supplying are South America, Asia, Oceania and Africa.
