History

FAIR SUN INDUSTRIAL CO. LTD. was established in 1980 specialized in Automotive Switches, Door Lock Actuators, Solenoids, Relays, Clock Springs, Sensors, and other electrical auto parts to cover many different car brands. For over 30 years, Fair Sun has been focused on applying the best production technologies to maintain our customers satisfaction with the highest quality standards.

With over 30 years manufacturing experience and IAFT16949 certification, Fair Sun has became Tier 1 & Tier 2 supplier to OE/OES customers in North America and Europe from 2004.
